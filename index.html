About UGC NET Exam

📋 Exam Pattern

  • Paper I: 50 Questions (100 Marks) – General/Teaching Aptitude
  • Paper II: 100 Questions (200 Marks) – Subject Specific
  • Total: 150 Questions, 300 Marks
  • Duration: 3 Hours (Single Session)
  • Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Marking: +2 for correct, 0 for wrong

✅ Key Information

  • Conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA)
  • Eligibility: Master's degree with 55% marks
  • Philosophy as main subject for Paper II
  • No negative marking in UGC NET
  • Qualifying marks vary by category
  • Result determines JRF and NET eligibility
